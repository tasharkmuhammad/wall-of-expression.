Community

The wall is designed to hold community expression over time. People can pause, read, respond, and return later to see how the space evolves. The goal is to make public voice visible in a way that feels accessible and shared.

How people engage

  • Read what others have shared and add a response.
  • Use benches to pause and reflect before engaging.
  • Return later and notice what has changed over time.
  • Share a short message, memory, or hope (guided by clear community rules).
Example prompt
“What do you want your city to remember?”
“What would you leave here for someone else to find?”
