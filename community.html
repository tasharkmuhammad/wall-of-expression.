Community Themes

The wall supports shared expression through four themes: Justice, Belonging, Community, and Equity.

Touch interaction (non-intrusive)

Interaction is simple: touch a tile to reveal imagery. People can participate briefly or stay to collaborate.

Why these themes matter

Justice

Encourages empathy and shared responsibility by reflecting the lives of others in the community.

Belonging

Invites collective creation of supportive, welcoming imagery that people recognize as “for us.”

Community

Visually celebrates diversity, collaboration, and public gathering through shared participation.

Equity

Accessible and intuitive interaction that welcomes all ages and backgrounds to engage equally.
